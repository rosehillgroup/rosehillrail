Rubber Edge Beams - Our Recommended Solution

Lightweight Innovation

Rosehill Rail's rubber edge beams provide a durable, lightweight alternative to traditional concrete solutions. Manufactured from 100% recycled tyre rubber and bonded using cutting-edge cold cure technology, these beams offer significant weight savings whilst maintaining exceptional durability.

With integrated drainage channels, non-spalling design, and strategic lifting points, our rubber edge beams deliver enhanced crossing support with improved sustainability credentials and reduced transport costs.

Key Advantages:

Network Rail acceptance, quick-fit installation system, and compatibility with all crossing systems make rubber edge beams the preferred choice for most applications.

Why Choose Our Edge Beams?

Sustainable Materials

Rubber edge beams use 100% recycled tyre rubber, delivering improved sustainability credentials and environmental benefits.

Quick Installation

Quick-fit installation system with strategic lifting points reduces installation time and associated labour costs.

Enhanced Durability

Non-spalling design prevents cracking and maintains structural integrity throughout the extended service life.

Reduced Transport Costs

Built-in drainage channels prevent water accumulation and reduce maintenance requirements over time.

Universal Compatibility
