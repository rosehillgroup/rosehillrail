FAST INSTALLATION

Baseplate System

Versatile rubber crossing panels that can be cut to fit around fishplates, check rails, and turnouts for rapid deployment.

  • ✓Cut-to-fit capability
  • ✓Extremely quick installation
  • ✓No specific tools required
  • ✓Multiple grade options

Cut-to-Fit Flexibility

Ultimate Adaptability

The Baseplate system's solid rubber panels can be cut to fit around fishplates, check rails (single and double), and turnouts, making them especially useful for tramway applications and tight curves.

This flexibility allows for very fast installation and makes the system ideal for average sized crossings across various applications from track access and depots to agricultural and pedestrian use.

Modification Friendly:

Panels can be modified for cut-to-fit installations, and individual panels can be removed and replaced without dislodging neighbouring panels.

Key Features

  • Can be cut to fit around complex infrastructure
  • Perfect for tramway applications and tight curves
  • 1.8 metre standard length, can be cut in half
  • Simple baseplate pulls panels together securely
  • Compatible with all edge beam types

    Complies with anti-skid requirements

Technical Specifications

The Baseplate System provides maximum flexibility for crossing installations where standard solutions may not fit. Designed for average sized crossings, this system excels in situations requiring adaptation to existing infrastructure.

Each panel is manufactured to 1.8 meters and can be cut in half for shorter crossings, providing unprecedented installation flexibility while maintaining structural integrity and safety compliance.
