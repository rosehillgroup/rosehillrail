StrandLock
Level Crossing System

Locked-in stability for the most demanding rail crossings. Post-tensioned steel strand technology delivers exceptional structural integrity.

Request Technical Consultation How It Works

What is StrandLock?

StrandLock builds on the familiar Rosehill Baseplate installation process, with one key enhancement. Integrated channels within the panels allow a continuous, greased and sheathed steel strand to be threaded through the crossing once installation is complete.

The strand is anchored at both ends and tensioned using a hydraulic jack, locking the entire crossing assembly firmly into place. This mechanical locking action significantly increases stability.

  1. 1
    Install Panels Baseplate panels are installed using standard Rosehill Rail methods
  2. 2
    Thread Strand Steel strand is threaded through the integrated panel channels
  3. 3
    Tension & Lock Anchors installed and strand tensioned to specified load
  4. 4
    Seal & Protect Strand sealed and protected to prevent water ingress

Key Benefits

Enhanced Structural Performance

The tensioned strand links every panel, improving load transfer and reducing relative movement between components. The result is a locked, unified crossing structure that resists panel migration and maintains alignment over time.

Greater Design Flexibility

StrandLock accommodates crossing angles and layouts that would otherwise prevent the use of a standard baseplate solution. Particularly suited to higher crossing angles and complex geometries.

Reduced Maintenance Risk

By locking panels together, joint wear and long-term movement are minimised, helping maintain ride quality and geometry throughout the service life of the crossing.

Familiar Installation Process

Installers benefit from a process that closely mirrors existing Baseplate installations, with minimal additional steps or specialist equipment required for the strand tensioning operation.

Compatible by Design

Fully compatible with Rosehill Rail gauge panels, field panels, and edge beam solutions. Integrates seamlessly into existing rail infrastructure specifications.

Long-Term Dimensional Stability

Critical for locations where maintaining precise alignment is essential. The post-tensioning system ensures the crossing maintains its geometry under repeated dynamic loading.

Heavy Rail Crossings

Engineered for main line railway crossings where structural integrity under high-frequency, heavy axle loads is essential. The unified structure resists the dynamic forces that cause conventional crossings to degrade.

  • Higher crossing angles accommodated
  • Increased vibration resistance
  • Long-term dimensional stability

Tramway & Light Rail

Ideal for urban tramway systems where aesthetics, ride quality, and minimal maintenance disruption are priorities. The locked panel system maintains smooth running surfaces across the service life.

  • Superior ride quality maintained
  • Minimised joint wear
  • Reduced maintenance frequency

High-Traffic Road Crossings

Designed to withstand combined rail and heavy road vehicle loading. The post-tensioned system distributes loads across the entire crossing structure, preventing localised stress damage.

  • HGV traffic rated
  • Improved load distribution
  • Extended service life
