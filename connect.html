CONNECT System

Innovative dimensional stability solution designed for heavy traffic and acute-angle crossings.

  • ✓Ideal for very long crossings
  • ✓Perfect for acute road angles
  • ✓Individual panel removal
  • ✓100% recycled materials

Why Choose CONNECT?

Integrated Matrix Design

Unified system with simple bolt connections ensures stability and prevents panel displacement under heavy loads.

Quick Installation

Rapid deployment reduces track possession times and minimises service disruption for busy rail networks.

Selective Maintenance

Individual panels can be removed and replaced without dismantling the entire crossing system.

Sustainable Solution

Manufactured from 100% recycled rubber with unique polyurethane bonding technology.

Universal Compatibility

Adaptable to all gauge types, rail profiles, and clips while maintaining dimensional stability.

Cost Effective

Lower lifecycle maintenance costs and simplified inventory management reduce total ownership costs.

Technical Specifications

The CONNECT System provides dimensional stability at any angle in all operating conditions. Designed specifically for heavy traffic scenarios, this system excels in very long crossings and those positioned at acute angles to the road.

Each panel is compression-manufactured to ensure consistent performance and features our unique cold-cure polyurethane bonding technology for superior durability and environmental performance.

CONNECT System Specifications
Panel Length 1800mm (standardised)
Sleeper Compatibility 600mm centres
