Enhanced Deterrence Technology

Conical Stud Design

Our unique anti-trespass panels feature a sophisticated conical stud design that provides enhanced deterrence while reducing installation time compared to traditional security solutions.

Manufactured from 100% recycled rubber, these panels provide an environmentally responsible solution for railway safety while delivering proven results in reducing unauthorised track access.

Network Rail Approved:

Three distinct panel variations approved by Network Rail ensure compliance and effectiveness across different railway infrastructure requirements.

Panel Variations

Double Flange
For four-foot gauge applications (1490mm × 1300mm × 147mm, 116kg)
Single Flange
For outside four-foot applications (1487mm × 1300mm × 147mm, 110kg)
Flangeless
Full-width for platform applications (1484mm × 1300mm × 147mm, 98kg)

Technical Specifications

Anti-Trespass Panels are engineered to provide maximum deterrent effect while maintaining ease of installation and environmental sustainability. The modular construction allows for flexible deployment across various railway environments.

Each panel features conical stud design for enhanced deterrence and is manufactured from 100% recycled rubber with self-draining configuration to minimise maintenance requirements.
